Meeting Notes and Content Ingest Automation

A repeatable workflow for turning messy transcripts, notes, and source material into structured summaries, actions, links, and reusable outputs.

Problem

Meeting transcripts and notes are usually noisy. The useful parts are mixed with repetition, uncertainty, and filler, which makes follow-up slow and inconsistent.

Build

I developed a repeatable workflow that turns transcripts, notes, and source material into concise summaries, actions, links, and reusable knowledge blocks. The recurring requirement was plain-text outputs that are easy to paste into work tools and separate action items from general discussion.

Operational Shape

  • Take in a messy transcript, note dump, or copied meeting text
  • Run a structured summarisation pattern
  • Separate summary from action items
  • Preserve owners and deadlines when present
  • Keep uncertainty explicit instead of inventing detail

Why It Matters

This project sits right at the intersection of AI usefulness and operational practicality. It is not about a model demo. It is about reducing repeated manual cleanup work and making follow-up information more usable.
