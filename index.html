Knowledge System

Personal Second Brain / LLM Wiki

A local knowledge system that keeps raw source material separate from compiled knowledge.

  • Problem: useful information gets trapped in one-off chats, notes, and transcripts.
  • Build: a Markdown-based system with source summaries, project pages, indexing, provenance links, and an append-only operations log.
  • Why it matters: shows practical experience in grounding, structure, documentation quality, and information reuse.
  • Tools: local LLM workflows, Markdown, Git-style thinking, source preservation.
Microsoft 365 Workflow

Service Desk Knowledge Workflow

A SharePoint-based concept for turning repeated support issues into reviewed, reusable knowledge.

  • Problem: troubleshooting knowledge is often repeated, hard to find, or stuck in ticket history.
  • Build: a workflow using structured handoff records, SharePoint organisation, review queues, and Copilot-style grounding ideas.
  • Guardrails: human review, governed knowledge sources, and operational fit for a real workplace environment.
  • Tools: Microsoft 365, SharePoint, workflow design, service desk process thinking.
Automation

Meeting Notes and Content Ingest Automation

Repeatable workflows for turning messy source material into structured, usable outputs.
