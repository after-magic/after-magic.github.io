Featured Projects

Knowledge Engineering

Personal Second Brain / LLM Wiki

A local knowledge system that keeps raw source material separate from compiled knowledge.

  • Problem: useful information gets trapped in one-off chats, notes, screenshots, and transcripts.
  • Build: a Markdown-based system with source summaries, project pages, indexing, provenance links, and an append-only operations log.
  • Why it matters: demonstrates grounding, structure, documentation quality, tool use, and information reuse.
  • Relevant pattern: a working example of AI-assisted knowledge engineering rather than a one-off prompt demo.
Microsoft 365 / SharePoint

Service Desk Knowledge Workflow Proposal

A SharePoint and Copilot-style concept for turning repeated support issues into reviewed, reusable knowledge.

  • Problem: troubleshooting knowledge is often repeated, hard to find, or stuck in ticket history.
  • Build: a workflow using structured handoff records, SharePoint organisation, review queues, curator-agent concepts, and human approval.
  • Guardrails: governed knowledge sources, sanitised handoffs, review before publication, and knowledge-health checks.
  • Why it matters: shows how I think about AI systems inside a real workplace, not just in personal experiments.
Automation

Meeting Notes and Content Ingest Automation

Repeatable workflows for turning messy source material into structured, usable outputs.

  • Problem: notes, transcripts, and source material are usually time-consuming to clean and reuse.
  • Build: automation patterns that extract summaries, actions, links, timestamps, and reusable knowledge blocks.
  • Result: clearer outputs, better follow-up, and less repeated manual formatting.
  • Why it matters: combines prompt design, structure, and operational usefulness in a practical workflow.
Workflow Improvement

Automation and Dashboard Workflows

Practical workflow concepts using reporting, automation, and structured review flows.

  • Build: Power Automate, Power Query, Excel, Google Sheets, and structured data capture patterns for operational visibility and process improvement.
  • Context: this sits between support operations and AI engineering because useful automation depends on reliable inputs, review, and usable outputs.
